About Us!

STEM Roots is a nonprofit organization formed to engage kids, in the wonders of STEM as school budgets shrink, and younger students are left feeling unsatisfied with the level of science taught in the classroom. Our free workshops touch upon each area of STEM, including areas such as coding and engineering, as well as fairly complex concepts and experiments we believe are important to the growth of young scientists. If you are interested in volunteering for our club, please contact us!

Our Workshops!

Our bimonthly workshops are primarily held at the Rancho Penasquitos Library and cover every area of STEM as we engage girls in fun experiments in biology, chemistry, physics, coding, and engineering. They'll review concepts they learned in school, and go deeper than what is taught in classrooms. Please press the signup button to learn more about current and upcoming workshops and contact us below if you would like join our mailing list for more information!
