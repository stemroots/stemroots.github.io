About Us

STEM Roots is a nonprofit organization formed to engage kids, in the wonders of STEM as school budgets shrink, and younger students are left feeling unsatisfied with the level of science taught in the classroom. Our free workshops touch upon each area of STEM, including areas such as coding and engineering, as well as fairly complex concepts and experiments we believe are important to the growth of young scientists. If you are interested in volunteering for our club, please contact us!

Our Workshops

Our bimonthly workshops are primarily held at the Rancho Penasquitos Library and cover every area of STEM as we engage girls in fun experiments in biology, chemistry, physics, coding, and engineering. They'll review concepts they learned in school, and go deeper than what is taught in classrooms. Please press the signup button below to learn more about current and upcoming workshops and contact us below if you would like join our mailing list for more information!

Past Events!

This is a gallery of our past events, primarily consisting of workshops from last summer. We are hoping to expand our program to other libraries as well as low-income communities!

July 22nd

This workshop was a mix of Chemistry and Physics as the girls learned about viscoscity through experimenting with Oobleck (a non-Newtonian fluid). In addition, the girls built balloon-powered cars and competed against each other to see who could catapult marshmallows the farthest!

July 30th!

This workshop primarily revolved around Computer Science as we introduced girls to the wonders of HTML and CSS by allowing them to program a simple website!

August 6th!

As part of our Engineering curriculum, we bought simple circuit kits to allow girls to explore the flow of electricity. LED lights were glowing, alarm sounds were playing, and everyone was having an amazing time!

August 13th!

In this workshop, we partnered with Coding the Future to teach the girls programming with Scratch. In addition, we demoed some chemistry experiments and ending our summer workshops with a pizza party!
